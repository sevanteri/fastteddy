It was a stormy night. Teddy was hurrying to the castle. Thank goodness I'm fast, he thought.

He arrived to the biggest hall of the castle only to find that the lord of time, Past..., had passed away and his princess Present had been kidnapped.

"Who is behind all of this?!", he shouted. The princess' maid told him that it was the Rabbit King's minions who barged right in and slew the lord of time and took the Princess.

I'm the fastest in the world and I was too late... How did this happen... Thankfully, Teddy is not only the fastest teddy in the world but also the teddiest of them all! He opened a portal to hell to go and fight the evil Rabbit King and his less evil rabbit minions.

And so Teddy was off to save the princess Present and her unborn child, Future.
